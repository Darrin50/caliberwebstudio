## Cluster Angle

Michigan med spas operate under the Corporate Practice of Medicine doctrine and Michigan Board of Medicine oversight — which creates specific obligations for what a med spa website can advertise, what must be disclosed, and how before/after photos and outcome claims can be legally framed.

Most med spa websites in Detroit ignore this entirely. This post covers the four compliance gaps CWS sees most often on Detroit med spa sites: missing medical director disclosure, unlawful outcome language, non-compliant before/after photo usage, and FTC advertising violations on service pages.

This cluster sits between the anchor (website design strategy) and the booking mistakes post (operational UX) by covering the legal layer — the one element that makes a med spa website genuinely trustworthy AND legally defensible. It strengthens the anchor's authority signals and opens a distinct conversion angle: CWS builds websites that protect the business, not just websites that look good.
