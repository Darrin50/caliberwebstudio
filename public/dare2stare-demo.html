What We Offer

Extensions That Speak for Themselves

Every set is custom-mapped to your eye shape, bone structure, and lifestyle. No two clients leave looking the same.

Classic Set

One extension per natural lash for a clean, natural look that enhances without overpowering. Perfect for first-timers and everyday elegance.

Natural Enhancement

Volume Set

Multiple lightweight extensions fanned onto each natural lash for dramatic fullness. The set that makes them ask, "Are those real?"

Full & Dramatic

Hybrid Set

The best of both worlds. Classic and volume blended for a textured, dimensional look — neither too natural nor too bold.

Textured & Dimensional

Lash Fill

Keep your set looking fresh. Fills replace shed extensions and maintain fullness. Recommended every 2–3 weeks for optimal retention.

Maintenance

Mega Volume

Ultra-dense, ultra-glamorous. Mega fans stacked for jaw-dropping, red-carpet fullness. For the woman who demands maximum impact.

Maximum Glam

Safe Removal

Gentle, professional removal of any set — applied here or elsewhere. Zero damage to your natural lashes when done right.

Safe & Gentle
