Our Services

Everything Your Pup Needs

Full-service grooming from nose to tail. Every dog gets one-on-one care with a groomer who's also a dog owner.

Full Grooming

Complete bath, haircut, blow-dry, and style. We take our time to make sure your pup looks and feels their absolute best.

Bath & Brush

Deep clean, premium shampoo, conditioning treatment, and thorough brushout. Perfect for maintaining that fresh, clean coat between full grooms.

Nail Trimming

Safe, careful nail trimming and filing. We handle even the most nervous pups with patience and gentle hands.

Ear Cleaning

Thorough ear cleaning to prevent infections and keep your pup comfortable. Gentle and safe for all breeds.

De-Shedding Treatment

Specialized de-shedding bath and brushout to reduce loose hair. Your furniture will thank you.

Puppy's First Groom

A gentle, patient introduction to grooming for puppies. We build positive associations so your pup grows up loving bath day.
