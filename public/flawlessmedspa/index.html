About Dr. Kalsi

Where medicine meets
aesthetic artistry.

"Health and well-being don't live in the body alone. They live in the mind and spirit too."

Dr. Julie Kalsi Beri, D.O. spent over two decades in internal medicine before founding Flawless Med Spa — bringing her physician-level precision to the world of aesthetic treatments. At Flawless, every procedure is approached with the same rigor and care as a clinical practice: proper patient evaluation, medical-grade protocols, and treatment plans that respect the whole person.

Dr. Kalsi is an active volunteer at Livonia's Hope Clinic and a member of the greater Livonia community. When you come to Flawless, you're not just getting a treatment — you're getting the expertise of a physician who genuinely cares about your wellbeing.
