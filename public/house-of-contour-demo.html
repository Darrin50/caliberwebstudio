What We Offer

Treatments that actually work.

Body Contouring

Non-invasive fat reduction and sculpting treatments. Real, measurable results in targeted areas — without surgery or downtime.

IV Hydration Therapy

Custom IV drip formulas for energy, immunity, recovery, and skin radiance. Administered by our RN in a clean, comfortable suite.

Injectables

Botox and dermal fillers administered by our registered nurse. Subtle, natural-looking results. Not frozen, not obvious — just refreshed.

Post-Op Lymphatic

Meet Your Practitioner

Stormie Anderson, RN
Founder & Lead Practitioner

Stormie Anderson built House of Contour from the ground up — not as a business, but as a mission. After years in nursing, she saw a gap: women in Detroit deserved access to the same world-class aesthetic treatments available in bigger markets, delivered by someone who actually understood their bodies and their stories.

Since opening, she has helped over 750 clients lose a combined 10,000 pounds, regain confidence, and transform their relationship with their bodies. Her work has earned recognition from the Detroit City Council, Forbes, and the White House — but she’ll tell you the only award that matters is a client seeing herself differently in the mirror.
