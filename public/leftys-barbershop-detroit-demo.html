Our Story

About Lefty's

Lefty's Barbershop is tucked above Electric Park Tattoo on Adelaide Street in the heart of Eastern Market — one of Detroit's most alive and real neighborhoods. From day one, the focus has been simple: classic cuts, done right, every time.

Pompadours, flattops, contours, fades — styles that have been around for generations because they work. We don't chase trends. We master the fundamentals and execute them clean.

Walk-ins are always welcome, but online booking means you can lock in your time and walk straight to the chair.
