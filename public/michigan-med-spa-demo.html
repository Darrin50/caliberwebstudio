What We Offer

Our Treatments

Advanced aesthetic medicine with physician oversight and the Icon™ laser system. Personalized plans for every skin type.

HydraFacial
Deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration in one treatment. Immediately visible results with zero downtime — glowing skin after a single session.
Laser Hair Removal
Permanent hair reduction using the Icon™ system. Safe for all skin types, treating face and body areas with lasting precision and comfort.
Scar Removal
The Icon™ fractional laser breaks down scar tissue and triggers natural healing. Effective on acne scars, surgical scars, and stretch marks.
Skin Rejuvenation
Icon™ treatments for brown spot removal, rosacea, facial veins, and texture improvement. Brighter, more even-toned skin with each session.
Botox® Injectables
Administered by Kelly D'Arcy RN, Master Injector. Precise, natural-looking results for forehead lines, crow's feet, and facial contouring.
Dermal Fillers
Hyaluronic acid fillers for lip augmentation, cheek volume, and facial contouring. Soft, natural results that restore youthful structure.
SculpSure Body Contouring
FDA-cleared laser treatment destroys stubborn fat cells in 25 minutes. No surgery, no downtime. Targets abdomen, flanks, back, and thighs.
Kybella & Skin Tightening
Eliminate submental fullness (double chin) permanently with Kybella, or tighten lax skin on face and body with our non-surgical lifting protocols.

Our Practice

Medicine Meets Aesthetics

Michigan Med Spa is a full-service medical spa in West Bloomfield, MI, operating under the physician supervision of Dr. Marvin Bleiberg, MD. We combine clinical precision with a luxury spa experience — the kind of results you can only get when a real doctor is guiding your care.

We use the Icon™ Aesthetic System — one of the most advanced laser platforms available — for treatments ranging from laser hair removal to scar reduction to skin rejuvenation. Our injectable treatments are performed exclusively by Kelly D'Arcy RN, Master Injector, with a focus on natural-looking enhancement.
