Common Questions

Frequently Asked

When should I schedule a Botox appointment?

We recommend scheduling Botox treatments 2–4 weeks before any major event to allow full results to develop. Results typically take effect within 3–7 days and last 3–4 months on average.

Can anyone get Botox?

Most healthy adults are candidates for Botox. Contraindications include pregnancy, breastfeeding, known allergy to botulinum toxin, or certain neuromuscular conditions. A consultation with Rachel will determine if Botox is right for you.

What's included in the $99/month membership?

Members receive priority booking, exclusive discounts on treatments, savings on Skinbetter Science retail products, and consistent aesthetic care at a flat monthly investment. Cancel anytime.

How long do dermal filler results last?

Results vary by product and placement, but most hyaluronic acid fillers last 9–18 months. Lip fillers typically last 6–12 months. Rachel will discuss expected duration during your consultation based on your specific treatment areas.
