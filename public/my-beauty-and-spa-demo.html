Every treatment is adjusted for your skin tone, Fitzpatrick type, and personal goals. Not guesswork — precision.

IPL Photofacial

Intense pulsed light targets sun spots, redness, and uneven tone. Settings carefully adjusted for melanin-rich and Asian skin to prevent hyperpigmentation.

Neurotoxins

Dermal Fillers

RF Microneedling

Radiofrequency energy delivered through microneedles to tighten skin, reduce acne scars, and stimulate collagen — with settings safe for darker skin tones.

Why It Matters

Most Medspas
Weren't Built for Your Skin.

Asian skin has unique characteristics — higher melanin density in certain layers, different aging patterns, elevated sensitivity to hyperpigmentation after treatment. Lily built this practice specifically around those realities.

Fitzpatrick I–VI Expertise

Treatment parameters calibrated per skin type. Laser and IPL settings that work safely on darker Asian skin tones — not just adjusted from a default preset.

Melasma & PIH Specialists

Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation and melasma are the #1 skin concern in Asian populations. We have dedicated protocols for both — not just standard brightening treatments.

Filler Placement for East Asian Anatomy

Midface volume, jawline contouring, and lip treatment require different injection points for East Asian facial structure. Lily is trained specifically in this approach.

Our Story

Built for the
Skin Troy Deserves

Lily opened My Beauty & Spa because she couldn't find a medspa in Troy that truly understood her skin — and she knew she wasn't alone. Troy has one of the largest Asian American communities in Michigan, and the local medspa market was built for someone else.

Every treatment offered here comes with protocols developed specifically for Asian skin: adjusted laser settings, hyperpigmentation-aware chemical peel sequences, and filler techniques calibrated to East Asian facial anatomy. Not approximations. Not defaults. Actual expertise.

Medical oversight is provided by Minghui Giebel, ensuring every treatment plan is clinical-grade from consultation through follow-up.
