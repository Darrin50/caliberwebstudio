## THE 7 BULLETS (most damaging findings)

### 1. Proweaver Trap — D+ BBB Rating, 18 Complaints
Your current website vendor, Proweaver, has an **D+ rating with the Better Business Bureau** and **18 filed complaints** — documented cases of billing overcharges and removing client websites over payment disputes. You have zero control over your own site. Every change requires a support ticket to an offshore team. A Caliber site is yours — full admin access, zero vendor lock-in.

### 2. Zero Meta Descriptions — On Every Single Page
Google uses your meta description as your "ad copy" in search results. Every single page on faithmedicalspa.com — homepage, about, contact, all 10 service pages — is missing its meta description. Google is writing your ad copy for you, and it's writing it badly. This alone is depressing your click-through rate on every organic search.

### 3. No Schema Markup = No Rich Results
Your competitors' listings in Google show star ratings, pricing, and service features. Yours shows plain text. That's because they have **JSON-LD schema markup** and you don't. Schema is free to add; it just has to be built in. You're invisible to the features that make Google listings stand out.

### 4. "Call Us" Is the Only CTA — In 2026
Every single button and call-to-action on your website says "Contact us for more details" or a phone number. **Med spa patients book online, at night, from their phones.** If they can't book at 10pm, they book somewhere else. Your WellnessLiving profile even shows "Online booking not available yet." That sentence is costing you appointments.

### 5. No Before/After Gallery
Before/after images are **the #1 conversion driver for aesthetic medicine**. Patients make their decision based on results. Your site has zero before/after photos — not one. Competitors who show real results from real patients in Livonia and the surrounding area are converting the exact patients you should be seeing.

### 6. robots.txt Blocks Google from Seeing Your Photos
There's a line in your website's robots.txt file that tells Google **not to crawl your photo uploads**. This means none of your images can appear in Google Image Search — for a visually-driven med spa, this is like having an invisible signage. One-line fix; massive impact.

### 7. Stock Photos Throughout — Zero Trust Signal
Every photo on your website — the "doctor," the "patient getting a facial," the before/after — came from Proweaver's stock image library. We know because the filenames follow Proweaver's internal naming convention. Patients seeking Morpheus8 or body contouring want to see your actual clinic, your actual results, your actual face. Stock photos tell a different story.

## SECONDARY BULLETS (for deeper conversation)

- **Gmail address in production** (`faithurgentcare@gmail.com`) — signals zero professional infrastructure to patients and to Google
- **Visitor counter in footer** — a live hit counter on a medical spa website reads as 2009 web design, not 2026 clinical excellence
- **Alt text typo:** your weight loss page has "wight loss" in an image alt attribute — it's indexed that way in Google
- **Financing page missing** — you accept CareCredit but advertise it nowhere; CareCredit acceptance is a conversion trigger for $1,500+ procedures
- **Contact page has no map, no hours, no form** — the worst contact page a local business can have
- **Insurance page lists no insurers** — a page that says "we accept insurance" without naming any carrier is worse than no page at all
- **Zero analytics** — you have no idea how many people visit your site, where they come from, or which services they're looking at
