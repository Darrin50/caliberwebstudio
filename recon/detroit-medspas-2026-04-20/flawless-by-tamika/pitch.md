## What We Found (Specific Audit Findings)

- **No before/after gallery exists.** The /gallery URL returns a 404 error. In the med spa industry, results photography is the single most powerful sales tool. Every serious competitor has one. Tamika does not.

- **The blog has zero relevant content.** The 4 blog posts on the site — all published June 2, 2025 — are default WordPress theme placeholders about travel packing, winter products for men, and gift guides. A potential client who clicks the blog sees a travel blog, not a med spa. This is an active credibility problem, not just a missed opportunity.

- **Title tags have no local keywords.** The homepage title is `Home - Flawless By Tamika Med Spa`. It contains no city, no state, no service keyword. Google uses title tags as a primary ranking signal. Every competitor is targeting "Livonia MI" and "med spa" in their titles. This site is not.

- **No LocalBusiness schema markup.** Google uses structured data to generate rich search results: star ratings, hours, phone numbers, and map placements. This site has zero schema markup on any page that matters, making it ineligible for these visibility features.

- **18+ services with no pricing.** Every service offered — from $50 dermaplaning to $500+ Sculptra injections — has a description but no price range. Modern med spa clients research pricing before they call. Competitors who publish pricing capture these clients first.

- **No Google Maps embed or location page.** There is no visible map, no embedded directions, no location landing page. For a business on Middlebelt Rd in Livonia, this is a missed local SEO signal and a friction point for new clients trying to find the clinic.

- **GoDaddy shared hosting = slow load times.** The site runs on GoDaddy's shared hosting infrastructure, which consistently underperforms on Google's Core Web Vitals metrics. Page speed is a direct Google ranking factor. Competitors on managed cloud hosting will outrank a slow-loading site regardless of content.

- **No mobile conversion features.** There is no sticky click-to-call button, no mobile-optimized booking bar, and no streamlined mobile CTA. Over 70% of local service searches happen on mobile. A client searching from their phone has no instant path to calling or booking.

- **The LLC was dissolved in 2021 and the domain is brand-new.** Michigan Secretary of State records show the original Flawless By Tamika LLC was incorporated in April 2018 and dissolved February 2021. The new domain was registered June 2025. Google is starting the domain age clock from zero — four years of potential history is gone.

- **Invisible in local rankings.** Flawless By Tamika does not appear in any aggregated "best medspa in Livonia" list (TrustAnalytica, Yelp top results). She is not competing in the local pack.
