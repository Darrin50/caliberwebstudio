## WHAT WE FOUND (The Art of War Brief)

We audited Flawless Med Spa's entire digital footprint before reaching out. Here's what the battlefield looks like:

### The Website Is Completely Down
Your website at flawlessmdspa.com is not loading for anyone — right now, today. When we ran diagnostics:
- **HTTPS fails entirely** — your SSL certificate is broken. Any visitor's browser shows "Your connection is not private." Most people click away immediately.
- **HTTP returns a 503 error** — served by a Chinese CDN (Alibaba Cloud). Your domain is essentially parked on Chinese infrastructure.
- **Google shows zero pages indexed** — your website is invisible to every search engine.

This isn't a minor bug. The site appears to be on a platform where monthly hosting fees lapsed, and the domain was re-registered in March 2025 but never properly set up. The domain itself is registered through a Chinese registrar (Gname 025 Inc.), routed through Chinese hosting, and returning server errors.

### Your GBP Website Link Goes Nowhere
Anyone who finds you on Google Maps and clicks "Website" gets a dead page — or a security warning. That's the moment they close the tab and book with a competitor.

Not on page 1, not on page 5, not anywhere. Your only discovery channel right now is your Google Business Profile, and even that sends visitors to a broken link.

### Your Credentials Are Hidden
You're a D.O. with 31 years of clinical experience, a Henry Ford Hospital affiliation, and a specialization in integrative medicine. That's the strongest trust story in the Livonia med spa market — and it's completely absent from your digital presence. Competitors are marketing Botox with far less medical authority than you have.

### Your Reputation Is Being Wasted
A 4.93-star rating across 43 reviews is exceptional — top 1% in your category. But with a dead website and no social media, the people who haven't already found you have no way to discover how good you are. That reputation exists only for people who already know you.
