## The Framing for the Conversation

**Don't open with "your website is bad."** Open with:

> "You have one of the most credentialed practices in Southfield — an MD, a Spravato certification, a loyal Instagram audience. But right now, someone searching 'botox southfield mi' on Google isn't finding you first. House of Contour is. We want to fix that."

**Pain points to probe:**
1. "How are most of your new patients finding you?" — If they say Instagram or word of mouth, acknowledge it, then explain the gap in search-driven acquisition.
2. "Have you noticed any patients mention they went elsewhere first?" — This surfaces competitive awareness.
3. "Do you know your Google review count compared to House of Contour?" — Use this to open the GBP conversation.
