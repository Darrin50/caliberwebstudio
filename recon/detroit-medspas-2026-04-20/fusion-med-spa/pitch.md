## OBJECTION HANDLING

### "I get enough clients already."
*"Absolutely — your reviews prove that. This isn't about getting different clients. It's about what happens when one of your 70K Instagram followers tries to book at midnight, or when someone Googles 'laser hair removal Dearborn Heights' and your competitor's site shows up first. Right now, every click that isn't a phone call is lost."*

### "I've been fine without a website for years."
*"You've been successful despite it — that's even more impressive. But here's what you're missing: your domain fusionspausa.com expires in December 2026. Once it expires again, a squatter buys it, and the next person who searches your business name finds a parking page. That's your brand — your 16 years of work — at the mercy of a domain broker."*

### "I manage everything through Instagram / Facebook."
*"Instagram is rented land. Mark Zuckerberg can change the algorithm tomorrow, disable your page, or add a competitor's ad right under your bio. A website is the one thing you own. We can make Instagram send people to your booking page automatically — but only if you have one."*

### "It's too expensive."
*"How many laser package clients do you need to book in a month to pay for a site that never sleeps? One? Maybe two? Your packages run $1,500+. This site pays for itself with the first booking it captures that would have otherwise called and hung up."*

### "I don't have time to manage a website."
*"You don't have to. We build it, launch it, and hand you the keys. The site runs itself — booking form, Google analytics, SEO — while you focus on the floor. Monthly maintenance is 30 minutes of your time, max."*
