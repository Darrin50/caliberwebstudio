## THE 10 WEAPONS (Specific Problems to Present)

### 1. Wix Is Sabotaging Your Google Rankings
Your service pages have URLs like `/services-9` and `/services-9-1`. Google can't tell what those pages are about. Your competitor The Warren Medspa ranks for "microneedling Warren MI" — you offer the same service but you're invisible. This isn't a content problem; it's a platform problem. Wix generates these slugs and they cannot be changed.

**Ask:** "Do you know what position you rank for 'facial Warren MI'? Let me show you theirs vs. yours."

### 2. Google Is Writing Your Sales Copy for You
4 of your 5 main pages have no meta description. That means when your site appears in Google, Google writes the preview text itself — and it doesn't know what makes you special. Every impression in Google search is a missed pitch opportunity.

### 3. You're Invisible for Your Highest-Value Services
You offer microneedling at $200+, hydrodermabrasion, and multiple chemical peel tiers. Those are high-intent searches with buyers who are ready to spend. But your site has zero content targeting those terms. Meanwhile, ZI BLOOM Aesthetics and The Warren Medspa own those keyword positions.

### 4. No Before/After Gallery = Leaving Money on the Table
For a business offering microneedling and clinical facials, before/after photos are the #1 conversion driver. Patients don't book a $200 treatment without seeing results. You have none. Zero. This page alone could double your booking rate on skin treatment services.

### 5. Your Credentials Are Hidden — and That's Costing You High-Value Clients
Kia has 17 years as a licensed massage therapist and a recent Aveda esthiology credential. That's legitimate expertise. But nowhere on your site does a patient see license numbers, provider credentials in a clear format, or any medical oversight framing. When someone is choosing between you and a provider who shows "RN, 15 years experience, license #MI123456" — they pick the one with the credentials on display. You have the experience; it's just invisible.

### 6. Your Domain Expires in 3 Months
kvitalityspa.com is registered through Wix and expires July 2026. If it lapses, your website goes dark and your Google rankings reset. A rebuild right now would include migrating the domain to proper DNS management — one less thing to worry about.

### 7. You Have 549 Reviews and 491 Instagram Followers — Something's Broken
That ratio tells me you have a loyalty and referral machine offline, but it's not converting online. A proper website with a clear Instagram → booking funnel, Reels content integration, and a real call-to-action hierarchy would close that gap.

### 8. Two Facebook Pages Split Your Reputation
You have @KVitalitySpa1 and @kvitalityspa3. Your reviews and followers are split between them. Merging them into one authoritative page consolidates your social proof.

### 9. Your Hours Are Inconsistent Across the Internet
Your website shows you open Mon–Thu. Multiple directories show Tuesday and Wednesday as CLOSED. Google's algorithm penalizes NAP inconsistency — it signals to Google that you might not be a real, maintained business. We fix all directory listings as part of a rebuild.

### 10. The Wix Footer Says "Proudly Created with Wix.com" to Every Visitor
Unless you're on a paid business plan, that footer stamp tells every potential client they're looking at a DIY website. First impressions matter — especially when you're charging $200 for microneedling.
