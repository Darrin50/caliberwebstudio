Use this section as a live demo script. Pull up the site and show each issue.

**The triplicated tagline:**

### "Your social media buttons send people to Squarespace, not you."

Click the Facebook or Instagram icons in the footer.

Two sets of social links exist on every page: her real ones, and the Squarespace template defaults (facebook.com/squarespace, instagram.com/squarespace) that were never removed. Depending on which icon a visitor clicks, they may land on Squarespace's corporate social profile instead of her business.

Show her: click the icon, see where it goes.

---

### "Five pages in your menu are 'Coming Soon' — and Google is indexing them."

Click: Laser, Sublime, Sublative, Hair Reduction, Mesotherapy in the nav.

All five are blank placeholder pages — but they're in the navigation, in the sitemap, and Google is crawling them as "thin content" pages. Same with the Cherry financing page she's advertising in the nav as a payment method. It says "Coming Soon."

Advertising a payment option that doesn't work yet is a trust issue. Google treating those five service pages as empty pages hurts her rankings.

## WHAT WE BUILD INSTEAD

### The Core Fix: Asian Skin Identity as the Homepage Hero

We lead with what makes her unique. The hero section communicates immediately:

> *Troy's only medspa specializing in all skin types — including Asian skin.*

Real imagery of Asian and diverse clientele (professional photography or AI-generated placeholders until she can shoot). Schema markup that includes `MedicalBusiness`, `ServiceArea`, and `FAQPage`. A homepage that ranks for "medspa Asian skin Troy MI."

### Clean Technical Execution

- **One domain, no duplicate pages** — `/home` redirect removed
- **No Squarespace platform tells** — no leaked template IDs in title tags
- **Real social links, no placeholders**
- **Contact form that works**, with Google Maps embed
- **Mobile click-to-call sticky bar** at the bottom on every page
- **Proper meta titles and descriptions** for every page
- **JSON-LD schema** on every page (LocalBusiness + Service + FAQ)
- **No "Coming Soon" pages** in navigation or sitemap

### Before/After Gallery — Done Right

Structured gallery section replacing the GIF:
- WebP images (5–10x smaller than GIF)

- Alt text on every image
- Diverse clientele shown

- "Melasma treatment for darker skin tones in Troy, MI"

These pull organic traffic from a market her competitors are ignoring.

### Professional Presentation
